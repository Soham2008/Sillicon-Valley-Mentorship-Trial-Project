Explore before Construct

Your home is likely the biggest investment you’ll make in your life, which
can also make it your biggest risk.

This may seem a bit scary but this app will guide you step-by-step through
the complex process of learning which goes by the basic construction of
house.

By learning what to expect well in advance, you’ll be able to develop a
budget that will cover all contingencies and ensure a relatively stress-free
time period while you wait for the day when you can move into your new
house and start making it your home.
